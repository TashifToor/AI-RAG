Learning Resources
Numerous resources are available for learning AI and ML with Python: 
Online Courses: Platforms like Coursera, Harvard University online courses, and freeCodeCamp offer structured learning paths from fundamentals to advanced concepts.
Tutorials and Documentation: Websites like W3Schools and [GeeksforGeeks](https://www.geeksfor Geeks.org/machine-learning/python-for-machine-learning/) provide tutorials, while official documentation for libraries like Pandas is readily available.
Hands-on Practice: Platforms like Kaggle offer datasets and competitions to build practical experience.
